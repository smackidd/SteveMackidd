Why now is the time

Living all my life in Alberta, I have been born and raised to suck at the teet of Oil&Gas. It has served us well. Like any commodity, it is not a stable industry. But when we prospered, we REALLY prospered.

Times are changing and Alberta is having to make some tough choices. Environmental concerns, a changing political landscape, industry surplus and the inability to distribute as easily as our competition has forced the industry's hand. It is time I look for more stability.

As we head in to the future, data is becoming increasingly more valuable. Infact, it will soon become a top three most valuable commodity in the world. I want a piece of that pie. Which is why now is the time to jump the sinking ship of Oil&Gas and move over to the first class lifestyle of Software Development.
